# Eden Field: Executive Summary (One-Pager)

## What Is It?
A modern, secure, offline-capable sync framework that replaces custom development for federal systems.

## The Problem & The Opportunity

### Today's Federal IT Reality
- Each agency builds their own sync/data systems independently
- **Cost**: $7-10M per system + $3.1M/year operations
- **Time**: 18-24 months to deployment
- **Risk**: Higher vulnerability, incidents, downtime

### The Solution
Use the **open-source Eden Field framework** (already built) as the foundation for all federal systems.
